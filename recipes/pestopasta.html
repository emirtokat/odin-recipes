Pesto Pasta

Description

Pesto pasta is a classic Italian dish that's perfect for a quick and easy weeknight dinner. This flavorful recipe is made with fresh basil pesto, which is bursting with aromatic herbs and garlic, and it's combined with al dente pasta to create a delicious and satisfying meal. The dish can be enjoyed hot or cold, making it perfect for leftovers or meal prep. The pesto sauce also adds a touch of creaminess to the pasta, making it a great alternative to traditional tomato-based sauces. With just a few simple ingredients and 15 minutes of prep time, this recipe is a great way to bring a taste of Italy to your dinner table. Whether you're cooking for one or feeding a family, this pesto pasta recipe is sure to be a hit!

Ingredients

  • 1 (16 ounce) package pasta
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • ½ cup chopped onion
  • 2 ½ tablespoons pesto
  • salt to taste
  • ground black pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons grated Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Fill a large pot with lightly salted water and bring to a rolling boil. Stir in pasta and return to a boil. Cook pasta uncovered, stirring occasionally, until tender yet firm to the bite, about 8 to 10 minutes. Drain and transfer into a large bowl.
  2. Meanwhile, heat oil in a frying pan over medium-low heat. Add onion; cook and stir until softened, about 3 minutes. Stir in pesto, salt, and pepper until warmed through.
  3. Add pesto mixture to hot pasta; stir in grated cheese and toss well to coat.
